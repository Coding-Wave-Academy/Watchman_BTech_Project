How it works
From packet to proof in seconds
Every network flow passes through a trained ML model, gets classified, and is permanently recorded on a local blockchain.
01
Capture
Scapy monitors your network interface in real time, aggregating flows into 78 statistical features per connection.
02
Detect
A Random Forest classifier identifies DoS, Brute Force, Port Scans, and Anomalies with 99.88% accuracy on CICIDS2017.
03
Record
Every confirmed attack is hashed and logged to a Solidity smart contract on a local Ethereum node — immutable by design.
04
Respond
High-confidence attacks trigger automatic IP blocking via iptables (Professional plan). Alerts appear on your dashboard instantly.
